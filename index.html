About Asteroid Prospector

Asteroid Prospector is a 2D browser based game set in the 30th century after humanity has expanded its reach beyond Earth. To support the expanding population of the Mars colony humanity has turned to asteroids as a source of raw materials. You are one of many freelance asteroid miners, using your ship to mine vital resources from the numerous asteroids hurtling across the Solar system.

Asteroid Prospector is being developed as part of the 2014 NASA Space Apps Challenge.

This page will be undergoing frequent updates throughout the Space Apps Challenge, check back regularly to try out the latest version of the game!
